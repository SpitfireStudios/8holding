Hospitality, Leisure & Gaming

Hotels and leisure-related enterprises are experiencing particular challenges in the current economic climate and Covid pandemic. Even the once-booming gaming industry has taken a hit, increasing competition and putting a renewed focus on the bottom line for those in the gaming and leisure industries. Companies seeking to compete in this market face a daunting array of challenges, from fundamental licensing and franchising issues to project development and management concerns; all of them are continually looking for lucrative niche opportunities. 8 Holding helps them identify suitable opportunities compatible with our client's strategic expansion plan internationally and develop a plan to resolve the crisis.
