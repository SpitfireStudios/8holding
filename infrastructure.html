Infrastructure & Transport

The transportation and infrastructure industry faces considerable challenges in today's economy. Revenue margins are shrinking, brought on by the high cost of energy, labour and a host of other economic factors. Whether commuters, tourists or cargo shippers, industry consumers are demanding cheaper, faster transportation modes, either by land, air or sea. Regulatory constraints on the various sectors of the industry affect coalitions, state and local governments, and domestic and international corporations with interests in highway, road, and bridge construction, maintenance and improvement; all types of mass transit; passenger and cargo aviation and rail; maritime and seaports; motor carriers; traffic safety; hazardous materials; and pipelines. 8 Holding helps its partners in scouting business opportunities within this industry at an international level, leveraging local networks to identify exciting opportunities.
