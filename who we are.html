    Our Core Values

    There are absolute values and principles that we deeply care about at 8 Holding. They guide our behaviour and our expectations of ourselves.

    Integrity
    We take great pride in the tradition of integrity that we have developed over the years in our professional and personal life.

    Independence
    We are offering our clients independent, trusted and unbiased advice.

    Excellence
    We endeavour to deliver exceptional quality service to our clients, custom-tailored to their unique needs.

    Intellectual Capital
    Our network is our product, and intellectual capital is our principal asset.

    Clients
    We don't have clients; we have partners. And we are committed in heart and mind.

    Citizenship
    We are deeply aware of the importance of our conduct to our people, business partners, investors and the public at large. Above all, we must earn and maintain their trust in our daily endeavours.

    Diversity
    At 8 Holding, we firmly believe that diversity is essential to the firm's success, both for our clients and us. Diversity produces and inspires an environment that generates more creative thinking, better answers, and increased understanding. We recognize the importance of diversity in background, gender, education, culture, and life experience, which create multidimensional and rewarding work experience, one that simultaneously nurtures and challenges each other.

    Pro Bono

    8 Holding has a long-standing commitment to developing and delivering business services to people of limited means. We believe that we have a profound obligation to give back to the community. We do it so gladly and without any hesitation.

    We believe that pro bono work is good for the firm, improves the quality of our life, has a positive effect on professional skills and enhances professionalism.
