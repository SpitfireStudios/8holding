Financial Services

The current economic environment has created many challenges for the financial services industry. What began as a subprime mortgage crisis has evolved into a mortgage "meltdown", potentially affecting the performance of many types of mortgages, as well as residential, construction, and commercial real estate loans. The resulting decline in credit quality has adversely impacted many financial institutions' liquidity and capital levels. The financial services industry faces new and sometimes never seen obstacles and challenges. We provide strategic counsel to commercial and investment banks, insurance companies, broker-dealers, and other capital markets participants, including investment funds such as hedge funds and private equity funds in developing cross border synergies and identifying business opportunities across the globe. We remain an invaluable ally for our partners in the financial services industry, whether the markets are healthy or in turmoil and utterly different business environments.
