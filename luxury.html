Luxury & Fashion

A succession of trends changes the luxury and fashion landscape and opens new routes to profitable growth. At 8 Holding, we believe growth is no longer in the old back yard. Emerging markets will be a critical part of a consumer product company's long-term global strategy, especially in the fashion and luxury industries. We are ready to support our clients in entering local market infrastructures and integrating emerging market product strategies with their global strategy. Also, consolidation is forcing companies to be more sophisticated in their collaboration with retailers. The retail merger creates opportunities for developing strategic partnerships and localization strategies that transform results. Companies must learn what it takes to build winning brands in this changing environment. The path to winning brands starts with understanding the markets and their opportunities. At 8 Holding, we help our clients improve their performance by reducing costs, making their supply chain, marketing and trade more effectively, and creating new synergies through pertinent M&A activities.
