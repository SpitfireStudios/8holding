Governmental Relationships

Government relations refers to the sector of an organization that focuses on public policy and advocacy. An effective government relations approach requires an understanding of both the politics and the operational bureaucratic drivers of change. Both levels of relationship are critical to provide strong representation to the business community and balance the need for short-term change with long-term policy re-engineering. We understand government, legislative and regulatory processes and we have extensive experience in assisting clients in the resolution of issues arising from the complex relationships which evolve when business and government interact. We provide clients with a broad and diverse range of government relations and leverage our premier network of influential relationships with policymakers. 8 Holding helps her partners with strategic advice and comprehensive solutions.
